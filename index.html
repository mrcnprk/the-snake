How to play The Snake?

Use arrow keys on your keyboar to change direction of the snake.

Green boxes are the snake's food, when snake eats he is becoming longer.

Every portion of eated food increase your score.

Avoid hitting the border of the field, once u do that the game is over!

Longer the snake is, faster it moves.

Hit the space button to start the game! Good luck.
